Detailed Description

Rayleigh class

Represents the Rayleigh distribution, which is a specialization of the Weibull distribution. The Rayleigh distribution represents the absolute magnitude of the combination of two orthogonal, normally-distributed and iid directional components.

For more information, see: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rayleigh_distribution
